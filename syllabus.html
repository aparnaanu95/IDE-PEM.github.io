Assessment Pattern

Component Marks
Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE) 40
End Semester Examination (ESE) 60
Total 100

CIE Pattern

  • Review article based on publications – 15 marks
  • Course based task / Seminar – 15 marks
  • Test paper – 10 marks

End Semester Examination

The examination consists of Part A and Part B. Part A contains short answer questions from each module. Part B contains long answer questions where students answer any five questions.
